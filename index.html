Our Story

Yasa Traders began as a small family business in the heart of Kathmandu valley. For generations, our family has been preserving traditional Nepalese crafts and sharing them with the world.

Today, we work with over 100 local artisans who create each product by hand using techniques passed down through generations. Every purchase supports these skilled craftspeople and helps preserve Nepal's rich cultural heritage.

We believe in fair trade practices, sustainable sourcing, and maintaining the authentic quality that these traditional items deserve.
